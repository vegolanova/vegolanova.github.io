Project 2: Uber Data Analytics

  • Power BI
  • DAX
  • M Language

Project Overview

To create truly comprehensive and insightful dashboard, I had to get creative with the relevant measures and look at the data from a CEO's perspective: "What metrics would I care about?". The goal of this project was to perform an end-to-end analysis of a Uber ride-booking service's operational and financial data. The objective was to transform a raw dataset into a dynamic, multi-page Power BI dashboard that delivers actionable insights on key performance indicators (KPIs), customer behavior, and cancellation patterns.

Key Features & Accomplishments

1. Data transformation (ETL): Cleaned and prepared raw booking data using Power Query. Created calculated columns to enrich the dataset, such as binning customer ratings and more insight into time: separate Date table with continuous dates, and additionally weekdays, seasons, part of the days (morning, afternoon...).

2. Advanced DAX calculations: Developed a suite of complex measures to analyze performance, including:

  • Time intelligence: A 30-day rolling average to smooth trends and compare against monthly totals.
  • Financial metrics: fare per km (as a ratio of sums), estimated lost revenue from cancellations, and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth.
  • Segment analysis: analyzed the "cancellation habits" of different customer rating groups.
  • Interactive design: designed a comprehensive, multi-page report with relevant filters and navigation, inspired by professional BI solutions.
